## License Notes

- **Metropolitan Museum of Art**: All images of public domain works are released under CC0.
- **Japanese photographs pre-1946**: Under Japanese copyright law, photographs taken before December 31, 1956 by authors who died before December 31, 1956 are in the public domain. All photographs here predate this threshold.
- **Ukiyo-e woodblock prints**: All artists died more than 70 years ago; works are in the public domain worldwide.
- **National Diet Library of Japan**: Digital reproductions of public domain works.
